I’m Arrjun Singh, one of the top entertainers in the Pacific Northwest and across the United States, known for turning every celebration into an unforgettable experience. Beyond the stage, I’m a singer-songwriter with over 20 singles on Apple Music and Spotify, as well as one album and one EP. I’m also the host of the Growing with Arrjun audio-video podcast on YouTube and Spotify, where I share stories and insights to inspire others.

Whether it’s MC’ing your big day, playing the dhol, creating captivating content, or simply making every moment memorable, my mission is to make your event bigger, better, and truly unforgettable.
